# Guide - Missing Features

This guide is for features that are intentionally not finished yet. It should help users and contributors understand whether something is planned, postponed, or outside the current scope.

## Feature status

- Available now, planned, under review, postponed, or out of scope.
- A short reason for anything that looks obvious but is not included yet.
- Links to open questions when the direction is unsettled.

## How to read it

- Missing does not always mean forgotten.
- Some features wait on file safety, sync, mobile limits, or extension permissions.
- The page should be updated after major roadmap decisions.

## Review checklist

- Users can tell the difference between planned and unsupported features.
- Each major gap links to the relevant product or quality note.
- The wording stays factual, not apologetic.
