# Iconography and Symbols

Iconography should help users recognize files, views, commands, states, and warnings at a glance. The system should be restrained, consistent, and readable at small sizes.

## Icon set

- Pages, folders, attachments, maps, collections, graph, search, backlinks, sync, extensions, settings, warnings, and unresolved links.
- Filled or stroked style chosen once and used consistently.
- Labels remain available so icons do not carry meaning alone.

## Usage rules

- Use the same symbol for the same concept across menus, panels, and docs.
- Avoid decorative icons in dense writing surfaces.
- Provide accessible labels for icon-only controls.

## Review checklist

- Icons do not replace text where clarity matters.
- Warning and permission symbols are consistent.
- The set works in light, dark, and high-contrast themes.
